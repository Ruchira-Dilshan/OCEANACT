Why life below water matters

The ocean sustains all life on Earth — here's what you can do about it.

Ocean Pollution Crisis

Over 8 million tonnes of plastic enter our oceans every year, threatening marine ecosystems and the food chain — including the fish on your plate.

  • Refuse single-use plastics daily
  • Join local beach clean-up drives
  • Support plastic-free businesses

Marine Biodiversity

More than 50% of the world's oxygen comes from the ocean. Protecting marine species means protecting the air we breathe and the climate we depend on.

  • Choose sustainably sourced seafood
  • Advocate for marine protected areas
  • Reduce carbon footprint at home

Students Taking Action

As students, we have real power: every choice, campaign, and conversation creates ripples of change. SDG 14 starts with awareness — and you're already here.

  • Educate peers about ocean health
  • Start or join an eco-society
  • Volunteer for conservation projects
